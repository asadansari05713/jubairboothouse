How to Measure Your Feet

Follow these simple steps to find your perfect shoe size

1
Place Your Foot

Stand on a piece of paper with your heel against a wall. Make sure your foot is flat and relaxed.

2
Mark the Length

Mark the longest part of your foot (usually the big toe) on the paper. Measure from heel to this mark.

3
Find Your Size

Use the measurement to find your size in our conversion chart below, then visit our showroom to try them on.

Sizing Tips

  • Measure both feet and use the larger measurement
  • Measure in the afternoon when feet are largest
  • Wear the same type of socks you'll wear with the shoes
  • Leave about 1/2 inch of space in front of your longest toe
  • Different brands may fit differently
  • Visit our showroom to try on shoes before purchasing
