Our Story

Since 2007, we have been offering customers the latest collections of branded footwear — from casual sneakers to stylish boots — all under one roof.

What started as a small family business in Sohasa mod Tekuatar Kushinagar, UP, India, has grown into one of the most trusted names in footwear retail. Our commitment to quality, comfort, and style has remained unchanged over the years.

We believe that everyone deserves to walk in comfort and style, which is why we carefully curate our collection to include the best brands and latest trends in footwear. Visit our showroom to experience the difference firsthand.
